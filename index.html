Commercial model and governance

Trust also comes from how the engagement is run after leads start flowing.

Governance matters. Validation, billing clarity and operating cadence should feel like signals of discipline, not small print.

Commercial terms

  • Prepayment model usually proposed for the first 3 months.
  • After the initial phase, the model transitions to NET30.
  • Legal VAT added to indicated prices.

Validation + billing

  • Monthly lead report from the client.
  • Gross, valid and invalid leads reconciled.
  • Invalid leads detailed by reason.
  • Invoice issued after valid lead confirmation.

Operating cadence

  • KPI meetings typically every 2 weeks at the start of the project, then monthly.
  • Goals, strategy and action plan reviewed continuously.
  • Campaign managers optimize on an ongoing basis.
